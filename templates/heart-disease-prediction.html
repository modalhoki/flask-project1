Coronary Heart Disease Prediction

Explanation
A prediction model for heart diseases is a tool that uses historical data and patterns to forecast the likelihood of someone developing heart-related health issues. It helps in assessing the risk of heart diseases in individuals based on factors such as age, gender, medical history, lifestyle choices, and other relevant information. By analyzing these factors, the prediction model provides valuable insights to healthcare professionals in identifying individuals who may be at a higher risk for heart diseases and can guide preventive measures and personalized treatment plans.
